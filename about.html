"Software freedom isn't just about code: it's about knowledge sharing, community building, and democratizing access to technology. Every contribution, no matter how small, strengthens the commons."

On Community

"Communities form around shared curiosity, not just shared problems. The best documentation answers questions people haven't asked yet. The best communities make asking safe."

"Break things. Fix them. Document what you learned. Share with others. Repeat. Formal education provides structure, but real learning happens in the gaps between theory and practice."

"Running your own servers teaches you more than any tutorial. Maintaining them teaches you even more. Knowing when to shut them down teaches you the most."
